Growth Strategies

Economic Growth Enabling Services (Infrastructure, Utilities and Digital Economy) The infrastructure and utilities priority area is comprised of Energy, Water & Sanitation and Transport sectors.

  • Growth Strategies
  • 03 Comments

This priority area focuses on: Employment creation by revamping the industry. Increase availability of dairy products and cooking oil from soya bean and cotton seed. Increase availability of locally manufactured medicines. Increase earnings from both agriculture and mineral beneficiation. Improve availability of locally produced stockfeed, farm implements and farm chemicals and fertilizers. Increase SME contribution to other value chains.

Special attention will go towards maintaining and repairing existing infrastructure and equipment; completing on-going and stalled projects. This will be achieved through: • Drilling of boreholes, • Replacement of antiquated water transportation equipment • Increasing water storage facilities and capacities. • Building adequate water harvesting facilities.

TTHZ focuses on but not limited to the following: Enhancing leadership development training; Supporting Youth participation in Agriculture and Mining, Enhancing investment in local cultural resources including tangible and intangible heritage such as traditional knowledge and skills, as well as music, dance, theatre and festivals;

Achieving sustainable, inclusive and equitable economic(GDP) growth by; i. Promoting the development, of new businesses, employment and job creation; ii. Strengthen Social Infrastructure and Social Protection Programmes; iii. Promote Good Governance and Corporate Social investment; iv. To modernise the economy through use of ICT and digital technology.
