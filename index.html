Tripple THZ(PVT) LTD, often referred to as TTTHZ, is a profit-making investment company that sources and provides capital to existing and non-existing entrepreneurial ventures within and outside the borders of Zimbabwe.

TTTHZ has evolved over the years since its formation back in 2017. It has been able to evolve and grow into an international investment company as it registered in the United Kingdom in early January 2018 and a few months later in Zimbabwe on the 14th day of March 2018.

Its operations, however, are not limited to these two countries but span over many countries in different continents as it has sources of investment funds and invests as well in industries that need development.
